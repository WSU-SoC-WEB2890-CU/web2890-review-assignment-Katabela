About Me

G'Day! I'm a design aficionado with a twist – born and bred in the land of kangaroos and Vegemite, Melbourne, Australia.

Armed with a Bachelor's degree in Communication Design from Monash University, I decided to embark on a wild adventure four years ago.

I packed my creativity and curiosity and jet-setted off to Ogden, Utah. Why, you ask? Well, because I wanted to flip into the world of college cheerleading (yes, you read that right!) while exploring the geekier side of design. It's all about balance, right?

In the beehive state, I donned my cheerleading pom-poms by day and dived headfirst into animation and design by night. Call me the design superhero with pom-poms – I'll craft a dazzling visual experience that will make your jaw drop and your heart do a backflip.

So, whether I'm bringing energy to the cheer squad or infusing life into pixels, I'm your go-to for design that's as dynamic as a cheer routine at halftime. Let's create something, shall we?
